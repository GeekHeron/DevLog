⚠️ The Disadvantages of Split-Stack Development

🤝 UI & Backend Severely Disconnected

Focusing only on backend Agent development results in a rigid frontend UI, failing to support the complex dynamic workflows and real-time data visualization required in Multi-Agent collaboration.

🚧 Poor Business Coupling

Generic SaaS solutions on the market cannot adapt to your unique Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs), forcing employees to manually copy and paste data between fragmented systems.

🔒 Fatal Data Privacy Leaks

Exposing core corporate assets (finance, clients, proprietary tech) to the public cloud is exposing your business completely naked. A secure and controllable full-stack private deployment is the only way out.

💸 Unaffordable Collaboration Costs

In outsourced teams, frontend, backend, and AI engineers often talk past each other. Long communication loops lead to project delays or even failures. A full-stack solo operator is far more efficient and stable.

Amazon Ads Autopilot & Competitor Monitoring Hub

• Core Pain Point: Amazon's daily operations face high-frequency price wars and the risk of uncontrolled ad ACoS metrics. Traditional ERPs suffer from delayed data and rigid rules, leading to low capital utilization.
• Full-Stack Solution: The bottom layer combines anti-fingerprint browsers such as AdsPower/Ziniao with the Playwright engine to seamlessly scrape frontend competitor prices despite risk controls (fine-grained monitoring within ±5% fluctuations). The core decision layer is entirely managed by the OpenClaw Agent (amazon_ads_autopilot), which automatically executes "ad toggling" and "budget adjustment" based on historical budgets and keyword lifecycles. The final results are delivered via highlighted dynamic reports pushed in real-time through Feishu Interactive Cards to operations heads, enabling an unattended workflow from "defensive monitoring" to "proactive strike."

End-to-End Inventory Risk Control & Business Collaboration System

• Core Pain Point: Resolving the data silo challenge of long-cycle stock inventory (such as outdated inventory age stats) and the high communication costs between departments (warehousing and operations).
• Full-Stack Solution: Built a Python-based scheduled automation parsing engine (supporting V2-level data risk filtering) that interfaces with the underlying APIs of Jijia ERP to fetch real-time inventory-to-sales ratios. The system deeply integrates Feishu Bitable as a distributed lightweight middle office, converting accumulated abnormal orders into visual charts and high-risk orange interactive cards targeted directly at @Core Owners. Management can issue liquidation or extension commands with a single click right on the card buttons inside Feishu chat boxes, bridging the final gap of business operations.
